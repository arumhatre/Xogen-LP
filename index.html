How does exosome-based hair treatment work?

During the treatment, exosomes are carefully released into the scalp through a non-surgical procedure. After the exosomes are released, they penetrate the skin and reach the targeted areas, where they start their action to latch onto damaged hair cells. Later, they get absorbed into the hair follicles and release the growth factors and essential nutrients and promote regeneration and regrowth . In addition to promoting hair growth, they increase blood circulation in the scalp and reduce inflammation.
